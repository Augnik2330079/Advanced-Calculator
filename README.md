# Advanced-Calculator
A comprehensive, feature-rich Android calculator built with Kotlin. This application combines standard calculation with scientific functions, AI-powered problem solving, unit conversion, financial tools, and real-time graphing.
🚀 Key Features
•
🤖 AI Math Solver: Integrated with Google Gemini Pro to provide step-by-step solutions to complex math problems.
•
📊 Dynamic Graphing: Visualize equations in real-time using a built-in coordinate system powered by MPAndroidChart.
•
📐 Scientific & Programming Mode:
◦
Trigonometry (Sin, Cos, Tan), Hyperbolic, and Inverse functions.
◦
Bitwise operations (AND, OR, XOR, NOT) and Radix conversion (HEX, BIN, OCT, DEC).
•
🔢 Matrix & Equation Solver:
◦
Calculate Determinants and Inverse Matrices (up to 3x3).
◦
Solve Quadratic equations with support for real and imaginary roots.
•
🗓️ Date & Age Tools: Calculate the exact difference between dates or determine your precise age in years.
•
💳 Finance & Health Calculators:
◦
EMI Calculator: Plan loans with principal, interest, and tenure.
◦
GST Calculator: Add or remove tax with one click.
◦
BMI Calculator: Health tracking based on height/weight.
◦
Discount & Tip: Quick calculations for shopping and dining.
•
📏 Universal Converter: Support for Length (Miles, KM, Feet), Weight (KG, Pounds, Ounces), and Temperature (Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin).
•
📸 OCR Ready: Architecture includes CameraX and ML Kit integration for future "Scan to Solve" capabilities.
🛠️ Tech Stack
•
Language: Kotlin
•
UI: Material Design 3, XML, ViewBinding
•
AI: Google Generative AI SDK (Gemini)
•
Graphics: MPAndroidChart
•
Camera: CameraX & ML Kit Text Recognition
•
Concurrency: Kotlin Coroutines & Lifecycle Scopes
